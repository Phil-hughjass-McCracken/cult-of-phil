Rituals & Rites

All who follow Phil must partake in the sacred rites. These rituals bind us in devotion, power, and questionable sanity.

The Awakening Chant

Each dawn, loyal Phillians recite Phil’s sacred mantra while staring into a mirror... or a void. Same difference.

The Binding of the Scroll

Followers must write their darkest secrets on parchment, roll it into a scroll, and place it beneath their pillow. Phil knows all.

The Candle of Doubt

Once a week, a single black candle is lit while reflecting on all who have betrayed the cult (looking at you, Greg).

The Great Silence

On the 13th of every month, members refrain from speech for 13 minutes at 13:13. Any sounds must be replaced with dramatic hand gestures or screams.

Feast of the Forgotten

A symbolic meal of unidentifiable food items to honor those who never made it past indoctrination.
